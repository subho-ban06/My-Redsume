About Me (Bio)

I’m Subhabrata Banerjee, an educator, AI & Robotics trainer, and product designer with over 14+ years of experience in emerging technologies, including Industrial IoT, UAVs, AI-ML, and Agri-Tech. My journey spans from designing STEAM labs, train the Indian Army, educational institutions, to train the students for national and international competitions like IRIS, Inspire Manak, and Vivo Ignite, International Skill Challenge. I specialize in microcontroller programming (ARM, AVR, PIC, Arduino, Raspberry Pi), UAV technology, and content development across AI, IoT, 3D printing, and more. Certified by IIT Kharagpur, AIM-NITI Ayog, CCL IIT Gandhinagar and LearnX Singapore, I’m committed to transforming education and product innovation for a smarter future. Currently, I serve as the AI-Robotics Trainer cum Atal Tinkering Lab In-charge at D.A.V. Model School, Durgapur, while pursuing my MCA in AI and Robotic Automation from Chandigarh University.
